Michigan's Great Lakes

The Great Lakes are made up of five interconnected lakes surrounding the state of Michigan. Together, the lakes hold the largest amount of freshwater in the world. To remember the names of the lakes, think of the acronym "HOMES".

Top Destinations

  1. Mackinac Island

    2. National Historic Landmark. No cars allowed. Transportation by horse and buggy or bike. Trails. Beaches. Homemade fudge.

  2. Traverse City

    3. Cherry capital of the world. 30+ wineries. Film festivals. Lake Michigan.

  3. Petosky

    4. Cottages and resorts. Cool rocks. Beaches. Marinas. Golfing.

  4. Isle Royal

    5. Largest island on Lake Superior. Forests. Clear water. No permanent residents. Wildlife.

  5. Detroit

    6. Motor City. Sports. Riverfront. Greektown.
